# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Introduction
Shruti Sadhana aims to bridge the gap between ancient Sanskrit oral traditions and modern digital learning by creating a guided chanting application.

## 2. Target Audience
- Devotees wanting to learn stotrams correctly.
- Language enthusiasts and students of Sanskrit.
- Practitioners of yoga, meditation, and chanting.

## 3. Key Features
- **Interactive Stotram Player**: Audio synced word-by-word or verse-by-verse.
- **Multilingual Script Support**: Switch between Devanagari, English transliteration (IAST), and local scripts.
- **Meaning & Commentary**: Access to word-by-word meanings and theological context.
- **Participatory Chanting Mode**: An interactive mode designed to practice chanting along.
