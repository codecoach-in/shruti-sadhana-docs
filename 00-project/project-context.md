# Project Context

Project: Shruti Sadhana

---

## Why This Project Exists

Many devotees wish to learn and regularly chant sacred stotras but face practical challenges:

- Authentic learning sources are fragmented.
- Learning is usually unstructured.
- Correct pronunciation is difficult to learn independently.
- Consistent practice is difficult to maintain.
- Most existing tools focus on playback rather than guided participation.

Shruti Sadhana exists to make traditional stotra learning, practice, and devotional participation accessible while preserving authenticity.

---

## Vision

Help every seeker chant correctly, confidently, and devotionally — bringing the temple learning experience into their homes.

---

## What Shruti Sadhana Is

A platform focused on:

- Learning sacred stotras
- Guided practice
- Participatory chanting
- Devotional parayana
- Personalized Puja Ghar experiences

---

## What Shruti Sadhana Is Not

- Sanskrit language learning platform
- Generic meditation application
- Generic audio streaming application
- Vedic chanting certification platform
- Traditional course marketplace

---

## Product Philosophy

### Learning Through Listening
Authentic recordings accelerate learning.

### Learning Through Repetition
Repeated practice builds confidence.

### Participation Over Consumption
Users should actively chant rather than passively listen.

### Devotion Through Experience
Technology should support devotional immersion rather than distract from it.

### Consistency Over Intensity
Small daily practice is more valuable than occasional intensive usage.
