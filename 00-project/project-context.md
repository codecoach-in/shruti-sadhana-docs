# Project Context

Project: Shruti Sadhana

Version: 1.0

---

## Why This Project Exists

Many devotees wish to learn and regularly chant sacred stotras, but often face practical challenges:

* Authentic recordings are scattered across multiple sources.
* Learning is usually unstructured.
* Long stotras are difficult to memorize.
* Consistent practice is difficult to maintain.
* Most available tools focus on playback rather than guided learning and participation.

Shruti Sadhana exists to make traditional stotra learning and practice more accessible while preserving its devotional nature.

---

## Vision

Help seekers learn, practice, and perform sacred stotra parayana with confidence, consistency, and devotion.

---

## What Shruti Sadhana Is

Shruti Sadhana is a platform for:

* Learning sacred stotras
* Practicing chanting regularly
* Participating in devotional parayana

The platform focuses specifically on stotra-based learning and practice.

---

## What Shruti Sadhana Is Not

Shruti Sadhana is not:

* A Sanskrit language learning platform
* A generic meditation application
* A generic audio streaming application
* A Vedic chanting training platform
* A traditional online course marketplace

---

## Product Philosophy

The project is built around a few core beliefs:

### Learning Through Listening

Authentic recordings are one of the most effective ways to learn stotras.

### Learning Through Repetition

Repeated listening and practice help users gain confidence and familiarity.

### Participation Over Consumption

Users should actively participate rather than passively consume audio content.

### Consistency Over Intensity

Small, regular practice sessions are more valuable than occasional intensive usage.

### Respect Traditional Structure

The traditional organization of stotras should be preserved wherever possible.

---

## Primary Audiences

### Existing Devotees

Users who already chant and seek consistency.

### Spiritual Seekers

Users learning stotras for the first time.

### Elderly Users

Users who prefer simple and guided experiences.

### Families

Parents and grandparents teaching younger generations.

### Young Professionals

Users seeking a structured devotional practice alongside daily life.

---

## Core Product Pillars

### Learning

Help users learn stotras in a structured manner.

### Practice

Help users build confidence through repetition and self-practice.

### Parayana

Help users participate in complete devotional chanting experiences.

---

## Long-Term Direction

Over time, the platform may expand to include:

* Additional stotras
* Expanded guru participation
* Advanced practice tools
* AI-assisted learning features
* Enhanced personalization

These are future opportunities and should not be interpreted as committed roadmap items.

---

## Success Definition

Shruti Sadhana is successful when users:

* Learn stotras more effectively.
* Practice more consistently.
* Complete more parayana sessions.
* Develop sustainable devotional habits.
* Feel connected to authentic learning traditions.
