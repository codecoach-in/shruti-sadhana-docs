# Illustration Guide: Sacred Manuscript Style

Purpose: Guidelines for creating and managing visual assets, graphics, and icon illustration styles in the Shruti Sadhana platform.

---

## 1. Visual Style

The graphic design of Shruti Sadhana utilizes a **Sacred Manuscript Illustration** style. The visual aesthetic mimics classical Indian hand-painted miniatures, palm-leaf drawings, and ancient manuscript ornaments. 

Cartoonish vectors, modern flat illustration packs, or neon graphics are strictly prohibited.

---

## 2. Key Characteristics

- **Warm Color Harmony**: Assets must align with the primary brand palette:
  - Warm Ivory (`#FDFBF7`) for paper backgrounds.
  - Muted Maroon (`#801D24`) for linework, borders, and structural contours.
  - Antique Gold (`#D4AF37`) for highlights, light glow effects, and sacred borders.
- **Organic Texture**: Graphic elements must exhibit a subtle hand-painted, paper-grain, or classical canvas texture. Avoid mathematically perfect vector paths; line thickness and shading should feel hand-drawn.
- **Soft Devotional Gradients**: Shading is achieved using soft, atmospheric gradients that replicate natural light, puja lamp flame glow (Deepam), and dhyana atmospheres.
- **Symbolic Over Realistic**: Prioritize classical spiritual symbolism (e.g., stylized lotus contours, geometric Sri Yantras) over high-detail realistic portraits.
- **Low Visual Weight**: Illustrations serve as background context and atmosphere. They must always use soft contrast thresholds to ensure they do not compete with the primary Sanskrit text.

## 4. Operational Rules & Constraints

- **Content Primacy**: Every illustration is a background context asset. If text overlapping occurs, the text must maintain a minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio against the illustration background.
- **Transparent Fades**: Artwork borders must fade softly into the Warm Ivory background (`#FDFBF7`) using organic shapes or soft radial masks rather than hard-cut boundaries.
- **Low Contrast Watermarks**: Large symbols (like the Sri Yantra background) must utilize extremely high transparency (e.g., `3%` to `5%` opacity) to act as subtle watermarks without complicating text legibility.
